Chocolate Banana Protein Shake

A smoothie that is high in protein without protein power

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 spoon oats
  • Nuts, any type of nuts (almonds, cashews)
  • 2 Dates
  • 1 spoon Cocoa Powder
  • 6 Ice Cubes (if you want it cold)

Directions:

  1. Get everything
  2. Blend everything in a blender
  3. Add ice cubes if you want it to be cold
  4. Enjoy!
