French Toast

French toast is a dish of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and often milk or cream, then pan-fried.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • 6 Bread Slices
  • 1 spoon Unsalted Butter

Directions:

  1. Get everything
  2. Whisk milk, eggs and salt together in a bowl
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium heat
  4. Dunk bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides
  5. Transfer to the skillet and cook until golden, 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Enjoy!
